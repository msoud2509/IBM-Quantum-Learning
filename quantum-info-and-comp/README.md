# Quantum Information & Computation
Otherwise known as the foundations of quantum computing.

This course series is broken down into 4 courses:
1. **Basics of Quantum Information** ([Course 1](./course-1))
2. **Fundamentals of Quantum Algorithms** ([Course 2](./course-2))
3. **General Formulation of Quantum Information** ([Course 3](./course-3))
4. **Foundations of Quantum Error Correction** ([Course 4](./course-4))

Each course will have its own sections/subsections as needed.
